Activities

Whale Watching

Whale watching is a popular activity for tourists to do when visiting in the summer. There are many places throughout the Cape that offer these trips. The most popular towns people go to for whale watching trips are Hyannis and Provincetown. These towns attract heavy amounts of tourists throughout the season and offer several tours each day.

Surfing

The most popular places for surfing lessons take place along the Outer Cape on the Cape Cod National Seashore where the waves are typically the largest. One of the most popular schools is Fun Seekers, located in Wellfleet. The beginner lessons offered typically last last 1-2 hours.

Shopping

Cape Cod offers tourists so many opportunities to shop in unique boutiques in all towns throughout the Cape. The most popular shopping towns are Hyannis, Chatham and Provincetown. These towns attract thousands of tourists and have town center shopping strips with dozens of small businesses.

Martha's Vineyard and Nantucket

Off the coast of Cape Cod are two small islands, Martha's Vineyard and Nantucket. Several ferry boat rides are offered daily at an affordable price to get to these islands from May to October each year. There are more ferry departures on the weekends all season and the daily rides increase during the busy summer months.

Most towns throughout the Lower Cape offer ferries to the islands out of the ports. The most popular port to Martha's Vineyard is out of Hyannis Port and the most popular to Nantucket is Falmouth Port.
